What is Next Step?

Next Step is a web-application designed to help prospective college students find out what school best fits them! Through the Magic of Data Science and Machine Learning Algorithms!

Our Inspiration

Concern for High School Graduates

Often, high school graduates are worried about one thing after graduation: JOBS

A college degree is often required to secure a good job today. This leads to major complications for high school graduates.

They have to take into consideration of things like:

  • Which college should they choose?
  • Which degree is the most rewarding?

    • The Common Issues

    Due to budget cuts, most schools lack the resources needed to assist students in choosing the right college.

    Over 1/3 of 5-17 year old students in America are first generation students (Balemian, Kara and Jing Feng).

    Most of them are unable to seek advice about colleges from family members since they’re the first ones to attempt such a milestone.

    Most of us thought of how nice it would have been to be presented with a list of ideal universities that’s been tailored to meet our needs. We’ve put together a team to do just that.


    Features

    Fit-Numbers

    Our predictive algorithm will generate a Fit-Number, which measures the overall compatibility, unique to the user, for each school based on the provided information (academics, extracurricular activities, school life, etc.) and our school database.

    The more criteria the school matches based on user profile, the higher the Fit Number.


    Bookmarks

    From the selection of schools presented in the center panel, users can bookmark schools they are interested in.

    These schools will display information such as the Fit-Number, the number of times it has been bookmarked by other students, and if there are upcoming events.
